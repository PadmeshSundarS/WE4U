WE 4 U

WE 4 U is a non-profitable organisation which focuses on building a better,brighter,more beautiful world for the leaders of tomorrow.We aim to nourish,educate and provide shelter for the poor and the needy.The organisation provides youngsters a platform to serve the society and bridge the gap between the educated and the uneducated.Our volunteers educate and mentor children from orphanages,slum and village commmunity centres across the country to give them a better future.


WE 4 U is located in South Kerala in the city of Trivandrum.It was established on September 5,2010 by a small group of friends with a strong passion to change today's society and help with the betterment of children who are in need.We encourage more and more people to volunteer at our organisation to join hands in helping the less fortunate.By doing so,it gives our volunteers more perspective and experience about the poor and the needy.We give children a safe haven for them to nourish and educate themselves.We try to mould them into young adults capable of looking after themselves and making their mark on this world.
